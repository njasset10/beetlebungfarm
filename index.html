The Farm

Beetlebung Farm is a historic 5+ acre farm located in the town center of Chilmark, MA on Marthaâ€™s Vineyard Island. Lovingly farmed by four generations of the Fischer Family, Beetlebung changed hands at the end of 2018. The Farm Group LLC, a group of year-round and seasonal Islanders committed to preserving the agrarian nature of Chilmark, assumed ownership of Beetlebung with the intention of refurbishing this iconic property into a prolific, small scale, biologically diverse organic farm while establishing an infrastructure to ensure success for many years to come. Each year, using regenerative principles and a low-tech approach to farming, we will be growing vegetables, herbs, flowers and berries on a 1+ acre cultivated field and will sell our harvest at our honor farm stand on the property.

About Us

At Beetlebung Farm, we believe in the viability of high productivity, ecological farming on a small plot of land. Using cultivation techniques that create efficiency and low tech tools that are good for both the soil and farmer, our goal is to promote a healthy ecosystem and life style for all.
