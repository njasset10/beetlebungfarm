About

In Fall 2018, a group of friends, passionate about local food, regenerative agriculture, and the community of Martha's Vineyard, created The Farm Group LLC. Their mission was to purchase a landmark 5-acre property in the heart of Chillmark, MA called Beetlebung farm. We hope to build upon the property's rich history and iullustrate that a Farm belongs to and thrives in the center of its community.
